Story of The Game

Its 2018 "Jenny" is a 22-year-old girl, a Texas International University graduate with a degree in electrical engineering, she lives a happy life with her family in Texas, one day they were having launch when suddenly it became dark like something ate the sun, and a strange ship appeared in the sky, strange creatures started falling from the ship wandering the streets killing people and destroying properties, taking control of nations around the world.

After few years from the invasion -year 2023- these creatures aka aliens after invading earth and establishing a new order of life, they divided earth into several factions and created what they call the game of survival, forcing every human over the age of 18 to enter the game and fight against humans of other factions. "Jenny" begins with her attempt to survive this game and win for the sake of her faction, she is in the faction 3.

Just before the game starts between her faction and faction 7, Jenny meets peter who will change the course of her life entirely, this is the point where the game starts, each choice you will be presented with have multiple outcomes, some of the choices might lead to Jenny death while others will help her save earthlings, as aliens call them, play the game to find out more.

About

The game's genre is action and it's interactive meaning during the course of the game you will be presented with multiple choices each choice will lead to different outcome be careful with your choice since some will end with death.
